📌 PROJECT OVERVIEW

Research Focus

This project explores personal email correspondence between Kenneth Lay (former CEO of Enron) and his daughter Elizabeth Lay from 1999–2002.

Scope

The original Enron Email Corpus contains 500K+ emails. This project is powered by a manually compiled subset of 47 emails drawn from the Lay family correspondence.

Key People

  • Rosie Flemming is Ken Lay's executive assistant. She always replies on his behalf (yes, even with his own daughter).
  • Elizabeth Lay uses three emails: elizabeth.lay@enron.com (work), lizard.ar@yahoo.com (personal), and ealvittor@yahoo.com (personal — married surname). There's also an anomaly hidden in there due to an unexplained blip.

Methods

The subset was manually pulled from the .csv version of the original email corpus. It only took a few hours to capture and clean.
